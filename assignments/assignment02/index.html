2024 Koenigsegg Gemera

The Koenigsegg Gemera is a revolutionary hypercar that combines incredible power with practicality. It features a hybrid powertrain that delivers astonishing performance while still being able to accommodate four passengers. The Gemera's futuristic design and advanced technology make it a standout in the world of high-performance vehicles. Its a car that truly pushes the boundaries of what a hypercar can be with a bled of extreme performance and everyday usability. With its darkmatter electric engine and an 8 cylinder engine it produces a combined 1700 horsepower making it one of the most powerful cars in the world.
